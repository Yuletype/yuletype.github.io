What is YuleType?

At Mobelux, we take pride in our ability to blend really great software with really great design, but pulling it off means our designers and developers have to speak each other's language. This holiday season, we're celebrating by getting the family together for 25 days of typographic experimentation. Each day will feature a type experiment with a holiday theme, fully designed and coded by an individual designer or developer.
